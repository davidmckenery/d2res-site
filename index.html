About

Independent broker-led advisory for apartment owners and investors

David McKenery
Texas Broker License #637408

David specializes in sales and transaction advisory of small apartment properties across Austin and Central Texas.

He is a Certified General Appraiser with a decade of multifamily valuation and market analysis experience. He provides clients with disciplined pricing guidance, underwriting insight, and practical transaction execution.

His approach combines brokerage execution with valuation-driven analysis, allowing clients to make informed decisions around pricing, timing, and positioning. David works directly with clients throughout the transaction process, delivering clear communication and accountable representation from initial valuation and pricing through closing.

In addition to multifamily, David advises clients on select residential transactions.

David holds a Master of Real Estate from Texas A&M University and a BBA from The University of Texas at Austin's McCombs School of Business.
